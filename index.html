TETT - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

TETT ("we", "our", or "the app") is a nutrition tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

To provide our services, TETT collects the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Body information: weight, height, age, gender, and body type
  • Nutrition data: food descriptions, calorie intake, protein, fat, and carbohydrate values
  • Water intake: daily water consumption logs
  • Fitness goals: your selected health and nutrition goals

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for app functionality:

  • Calculating personalized daily nutrition targets
  • Analyzing food descriptions using AI to estimate nutritional values
  • Displaying your daily progress and meal history

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the app's core features.

AI Food Analysis

When you describe a meal, the text is sent to a secure AI service to estimate nutritional values. Only the food description is sent — no personal information is included in this request.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is never used for tracking or advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

TETT is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
